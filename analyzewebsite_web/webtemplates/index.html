Crawling the site and running the analysis. This usually takes 60–120 seconds — keep this tab open; the document will download automatically.
What happens
  1. Fetches the homepage plus up to eight internal pages, favouring about, team, product, evidence, news, and investor pages.
  2. Scores eight fixed categories and identifies gaps, priorities, Reg D posture, and UX fixes.
  3. Renders a .docx with the TEN Capital footer, listing every page reviewed.

Findings come only from pages the crawler could read. Client-side-rendered content, PDFs, and anything behind a login are not reviewed.
